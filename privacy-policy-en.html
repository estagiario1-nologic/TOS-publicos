Data we collect

Agridac provides an application for browsing, publishing, and managing agricultural machinery rentals. To provide this service, we may process data provided by users and data generated through platform usage.

Account and profileName, email, phone number, tax ID, locality, language, theme, profile photo, email verification status, and user type.
Platform contentPublished machinery, photos, documents, bookings, messages, reviews, notifications, and plan/subscription history.

Payments

On the web, subscription payments may be processed by Stripe. Agridac may store technical identifiers related to payment, such as customer, subscription, or price identifiers. Full card details are processed by Stripe and are not stored by Agridac.

On Android and iOS, digital subscriptions must be processed by the respective store purchase systems when that feature is enabled.

Mobile app builds published on app stores must not send users to Stripe to buy digital subscriptions.

How we use data

We use data to create and authenticate accounts, manage profiles, allow machinery browsing and publishing, manage bookings, send messages, show notifications, provide support, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Sharing and subprocessors

Agridac uses technical providers to operate the platform, including Supabase for authentication, database, storage, and backend functions, and Stripe for web payments. When in-app purchases are enabled, Google Play and Apple may process data required for payment and subscription management.

Location

The app processes the locality/city entered by the user and the locality associated with machinery. It does not use the device GPS permission to collect precise location.

Retention

We keep data while the account is active or while it is necessary to operate the platform, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, comply with legal obligations, or keep records required by law.

Account and data deletion

Users can request account deletion inside the app, on the Profile screen, or through the Delete account page. Deletion removes data controlled by Agridac, except where retention is required for legal obligations, security, anti-fraud, or dispute resolution.

Security

We apply technical and organizational measures to protect data, including authentication, access controls, database policies, and providers specialized in secure infrastructure.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests about personal data, contact Agridac by email: agridac.suporte@protonmail.com.
