Delete Agridac Account

You can delete your account directly in the app or request deletion by email if you no longer have access to the app.

agridac.suporte@protonmail.com
Summary

Last updated: June 26, 2026.

Deletion removes the account and associated data controlled by Agridac, except where legal or security obligations require retention.

Delete the account in the app

This is the recommended method when you can still sign in.

  1. Open Agridac and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to the Profile screen.
  3. Select Delete account.
  4. Confirm the action to remove the account and associated data controlled by Agridac.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, send a request to agridac.suporte@protonmail.com from the email address linked to the account. Use the subject "Delete Agridac account".

To protect the account, Agridac may request additional information to confirm that the request comes from the account holder.

Data deleted

Deletion removes the user account and associated data controlled by Agridac, including profile data, published content, uploaded files, messages, notifications, and associated operational records, where applicable.

Data that may be retained

Some data may be retained for the period necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse or fraud, keep accounting records, or protect platform security.

Timeframe

Email requests are reviewed and processed within a reasonable timeframe after the account holder identity is validated.
